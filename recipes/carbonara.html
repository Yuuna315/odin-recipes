Carbonara

Ingredients

  • egg
  • parmigiana reggiano
  • bacon
  • peppar
  • salt
  • spaghetti

Steps

  1. cutting bacon
  2. grating the parmigiana reggiano with microplane
  3. wisking together the egg, cheese and pepper in a large bowl
  4. cooking pasta with salt
  5. scooping out 1 cup of pasta cooking water
  6. cooking bacon while the pasta is cooking
  7. tossing pasta in the pan with bacon
  8. transfering them into sauce bowl
  9. adding 1/2 cup pasta cooking water into the bowl
  10. Mixing them for around 30 seconds to 1 minute
  11. It's ready to eat!!!
