Avocado toast

Ingredients

  • slice of bread
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • Pinch of salt

Step

  1. Toast your slice of bread until golden and firm
  2. Remove the pit from your avocado
  3. Put it in a bowl and mash it up with a fork until it's as smooth as you like it
  4. Mix in a pinch of salt (about â…› teaspoon) and add more to taste, if desired.
  5. Spread avocado on top of your toast
